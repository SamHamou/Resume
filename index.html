Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is Gooads CV Generator really free?

Yes, absolutely! Gooads CV Generator is 100% free to use. There are no hidden charges and no need to sign up.

Is my data safe?

Your privacy is our priority. All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server.

What format can I download my CV in?

You can download your CV as a PDF file, which is the most universally accepted format for job applications.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation required! Gooads CV Generator works entirely in your web browser.

Can I edit my CV after downloading?

Yes! Your CV data is saved in your browser. Simply return to the CV builder to make changes.

What information should I include in my CV?

A strong CV typically includes: Contact information, professional summary, work experience, education, and relevant skills.
