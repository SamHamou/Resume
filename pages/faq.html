Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is Gooads CV Generator really free?+

Yes, absolutely! Gooads CV Generator is 100% free to use. There are no hidden charges and no need to sign up.

Is my data safe?+

Your privacy is our priority. All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server.

What format can I download my CV in?+

You can download your CV as a PDF file, which is the most universally accepted format for job applications.

Do I need to install any software?+

No installation required! Gooads CV Generator works entirely in your web browser.

Can I edit my CV after downloading?+

Yes! Your CV data is saved in your browser. Simply return to the CV builder to make changes.

What information should I include in my CV?+

A strong CV typically includes: Contact information, professional summary, work experience, education, and relevant skills.

Which template should I choose?+

It depends on your industry and target role. Classic, Modern, and Minimal are ATS-friendly and recommended for corporate applications. Executive suits senior professionals. Creative works well for design and media roles.

Can I use Gooads CV offline?+

Yes! After the initial page load, the CV builder works fully offline. All processing happens in your browser with no server requests.

Does it work on mobile devices?+

Absolutely. The CV builder is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. The form and preview are in a convenient tabbed layout on small screens.

How do I switch between English and Spanish?+

Use the language dropdown in the navigation bar. The entire interface, including the CV preview and PDF export, will switch languages instantly.

What is the difference between single-column and multi-column templates?+

Single-column templates (Classic, Modern, Minimal) are recommended for most applications as they are ATS-friendly and easy to read. Multi-column templates (Executive, Creative) offer more visual layout but may not parse correctly in all applicant tracking systems.

Do you store my CV data on your servers?+

No. Gooads CV Generator does not have servers. Your CV data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is uploaded or transmitted. Your data is yours and never leaves your device.
