# Join the Society

Welcome to the Finchley Horticultural Society! We welcome anyone with an interest in growing their own flowers, fruit or vegetables whatever their age or ability.

## Subscription Rates

Annual subscription rates:
- £8 individual standard
- £5 individual senior (over 60 years of age)

The subscription year is the calendar year 1st January – 31st December. If you join after 1st October your membership will be credited to the remainder of the current year and the whole of the following year.

## How to Apply

To become a Member print out and complete a [membership application form.](http://www.finchleyhs.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Membership-Application-Form-2022.pdf) Return this with your subscription to the FHS Membership Secretary, 40 Hutton Grove, Finchley, London N12 8DT.

## Benefits of Membership

- **Allotments**: Provision of allotments on three sites in Finchley.
- **Activities**: An annual programme of activities.
- **Garden Visits**: Visits to gardens of outstanding interest, often with our own guided tour.
- **Trading Hut**: Gardening products available at competitive rates at our Trading Hut on the Gordon Road Allotments. The Hut is open on Sunday mornings, 10.30am – 12.30pm during the spring and summer months.
- **Discount**: A 10% discount on plants and seeds at [Finchley Nurseries](http://www.finchleynurseries.net/) on production of an FHS membership card.
- **Social Events**: Social events including a barbecue.
- **Newsletter**: A regular newsletter "The Grapevine" with local news of interest to gardeners.
- **Plant Sale**: An annual plant sale in May offering a wide variety of good quality plants at very reasonable prices
