# Society Documents

This page contains important documents for Finchley Horticultural Society members including our constitution, rules, and official forms.

## Constitution and Rules

- [Society Constitution](http://www.finchleyhs.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/FHS-Constitution-2018.pdf) - Our founding document and governing principles
- [Society Rules](http://www.finchleyhs.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/FHS-Rules-2018.pdf) - Rules and regulations for members

## Member Documents

- [Member Handbook](http://www.finchleyhs.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/FHS-Handbook-2018-1.pdf) - Complete guide for members
- [Membership Application Form](http://www.finchleyhs.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Membership-Application-Form-2022.pdf) - Form for new members

## Allotment Information

- [Allotment Rules](http://www.finchleyhs.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/FHS-Allotment-Rules-2018.pdf) - Rules and regulations for allotment holders
- [Site Maps](http://www.finchleyhs.org/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/FHS-Site-Maps-2018.pdf) - Maps of our allotment sites

## Meeting Documents

Meeting minutes and agendas are distributed to members and available at our regular meetings. For access to recent meeting documents, please [contact us](/contact/).

## Annual Reports

Annual reports are presented at our AGM each March and copies are available to members upon request.
