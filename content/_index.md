## Welcome to Finchley Horticultural Society

![Finchley Horticultural Society](/Eden/society-main.jpg)

The Finchley Horticultural Society welcomes anyone in Finchley with an interest in gardening. Finchley is an area within London Borough of Barnet, North London – postal areas London N2, N3 and N12. We run local allotment sites which include facilities for the physically disabled and for schools. The Society has an active program of horticultural events.
