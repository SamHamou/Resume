## About The Grapevine

"The Grapevine" is the official newsletter of the Finchley Horticultural Society. First expanded in Spring 2011, it contains:

- Society news and updates
- Gardening tips and advice
- Member stories and achievements
- Event announcements
- Seasonal gardening guidance
- Plot competition results

## Subscribe

Members automatically receive copies of The Grapevine. To become a member and receive our newsletter, please visit our [membership page](/members/membership/).
